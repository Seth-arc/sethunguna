About me

Hi!

I am an Instructional and Learning Experience designer with just over 12 years of experience in higher education, holding an MA in Education with a focus on digital transformation in higher education.

My expertise spans developing training strategies, online learning platforms, curriculum, digital media and web development (Frontend). My background includes roles in e-course development and leading instructional design initiatives, supported by a solid foundation in economics and management.

My work is driven by a passion for leveraging data-driven insights to enhance educational outcomes, continually seeking professional development in project management and digital transformation.
